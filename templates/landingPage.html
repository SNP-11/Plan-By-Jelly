About Jelly

Hi! I'm the creator behind Jelly, a passionate developer who undestands the challenges middle schoolers face with organization and time management.

After watching countless students struggle with traditional planners and apps designed for adults, I realized there was a gap in the market for something truly designed for young minds—something that speaks their language and motivates them in ways that actually work.

Jelly was born from the belief that learning good habits should be engaging and rewarding. By combining gamification with proven organizational principles, we're helping the next generation build skills that will serve them for life.
